Welcome to Our Test Drive Facility

At Larry's Race Track Test Drive Center, we offer you the chance to get behind the wheel of high-performance Old school muscle cars and experience the thrill of speed. Whether you're a seasoned driver or a complete beginner, our professional instructors are here to guide you.

Our track features challenging turns, straightaways, and everything in between, providing the perfect environment to push the limits of these incredible machines.

At the Race Car Test Drive Center, our mission is to provide an unforgettable and exhilarating experience for car enthusiasts. We're dedicated to delivering high-performance test drives, professional training, and a commitment to safety.

Our History

Founded in 1994, our facility has been a hub for race car enthusiasts for over 30 years. We've continually evolved to offer cutting-edge vehicles and top-notch training.

Our passionate team of race instructors, mechanics, and event coordinators are dedicated to making your experience exceptional. With over 40 years of experience, we're here to guide and support you.

Safety First

At the Race Car Test Drive Center, safety is our top priority. We provide comprehensive training and ensure all drivers are equipped with the necessary safety gear. Our vehicles are meticulously maintained, and our tracks meet the highest safety standards.
